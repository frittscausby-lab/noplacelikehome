What's Inside

Practical Advice for Every Stage of the Real Estate Journey

Real estate is one of life's basic needs and one of the safest pathways to long-term financial stability. But the buying and selling process is nuanced, and the difference between a great outcome and a costly mistake often comes down to what you know going in.

No Place Like Home delivers no-nonsense guidance for first-time buyers, homeowners looking to sell, and investors at any level — all in an approachable, conversational style built on more than 15 years of covering the industry.

About the Author

Fritts Causby

Fritts Causby has specialized in creating real estate content since 2007. A freelance writer for the Greater Wilmington Business Journal, Wrightsville Beach Magazine, and several other publications, Fritts has covered the full sweep of the industry — from luxury properties and market trends to the practical decisions that shape everyday financial lives.

He has been fortunate to work with clients, agents, and industry professionals across the United States. Spending time with his daughter, mountain biking, surfing, and golfing are some of his other favorite pastimes.
